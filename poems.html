The Cat

Raimondi the Cat

Thy soft pur
Doth deter
The ice inside my heart

Chess

"Chess Piece" by Rubber Dragon is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0

In chess I live the piece mind
As I try to place this piece of mine
And my opponent makes a plan so clever
While I sit there piecing mine together.
